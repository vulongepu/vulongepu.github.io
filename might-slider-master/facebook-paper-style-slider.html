About this slider

Facebook Paper Style Slider Template

This slider created with mightySlider capabilities. But videos and images are from Facebook Paper page.

Whole slider, video player, video backgrounds, and all animated text layers are mightySlider's features. You can simply use videos or images in any sizes or insert multiple animated layers over each slide.

For better performance and customizability some mightySlider APIs were used.
